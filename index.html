Joby Aviation - Saving people an hour a day in their day-to-day travel

Joby Aviation

It’s getting more costly to get where we want to go. More than a billion people spend over one hour a day in traffic. Transportation emissions are one of the largest sources of pollution. Streets, highways, bridges and tunnels are increasingly expensive to maintain and build.
We aim to solve these problems by delivering safe and affordable air travel to everyone—while advancing the transition to sustainable transportation.
We're a venture-backed startup with offices in Santa Cruz, CA and San Carlos, CA.

Art

Festival of Lights - Lyon

Festival of Lights - Lyon

From December 5 to 8, 2014, the first year students of the I.D.E.A. (Innovation, Design, Entrepreneurship & Arts) of EMLYON Business School and Ecole Centrale de Lyon, offer visitors to come share an immersive, luminous and interactive experience, in the courtyard of the ESPE, Ecole Supérieure du Professorat and of Education in Lyon, on the Croix-Rousse plateau. "Neither plants, nor animals, nor minerals, everywhere on the planet, mushrooms coexist and interact with all forms of life while still remaining very mysterious. "
Inspired by the world of mycelium, and as part of an original pedagogy, students propose an interactive installation entitled "The filaments of knowledge".
Like their inspirer, symbiotes will take several forms: crystalline, angular, wired, other alveolar, soft sometimes soft, light, gas or floating ... The device will be scalable and explained to the public by the students present on the site.
Participation in the Festival of Lights of Lyon around the theme "The filaments of knowledge".
Conceptual scenario: "Coexistences"
